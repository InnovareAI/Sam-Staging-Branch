# Intent Data Providers - Buying Signal Intelligence Options

## What is Intent Data?

**Intent data** identifies companies actively researching solutions similar to yours by tracking:
- Website visits to competitor sites
- Content consumption patterns
- Search behavior and keywords
- Review site activity
- Social media engagement
- Job posting patterns for relevant roles

## **Recommendation: Start Without Enterprise Intent**

### **Why Skip Premium Intent Data Initially:**

1. **Cost:** $150-5,000/month vs $9.40 current stack
2. **LinkedIn Company Posts** = Real-time intent signals
3. **Job posting patterns** visible through LinkedIn
4. **Social mentions** trackable through basic tools
5. **Website visitor ID** through Clearbit cheaper option

### **Budget-Friendly Intent Alternatives:**

**Phase 1: Enhanced LinkedIn Monitoring ($9.40/month)**
- Company page posts = funding, hiring, product launches
- Job postings = growth signals  
- Leadership changes = buying opportunities
- Technology mentions in profiles

**Phase 2: Add Basic Social Listening ($99/month)**
- Monitor competitor mentions
- Track industry pain point discussions
- Identify technology evaluation conversations
- Real-time alerts for buying signals

**Phase 3: Website Visitor ID ($500/month)**
- Identify anonymous website visitors
- Track page engagement
- Score visitor behavior
- Trigger outreach based on activity

**Total Enhanced Intent Stack: $608/month**
- Still 80% cheaper than enterprise solutions
- More actionable, real-time signals
- Better integration with outreach workflows

## **When to Invest in Premium Intent Data:**

- **Revenue:** $2M+ ARR
- **Deal Size:** $50K+ ACV  
- **Sales Cycle:** 6+ months
- **Team Size:** 10+ sales reps
- **Current Performance:** 80%+ of quota

**Bottom Line:** For most B2B sales teams, LinkedIn company intelligence + basic social listening provides 80% of intent data value at 10% of the cost.
