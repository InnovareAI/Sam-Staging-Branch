# Personas Library
Version: v4.4 | Last Updated: 2025-09-09

## Founders / Co-Founders
- **Priorities**: Growth, fundraising, efficient GTM
- **Pain Points**: Manual prospecting, lack of bandwidth
- **Approach**: Focus on growth acceleration and time savings

## Sales / GTM Leads
- **Priorities**: Pipeline generation, conversion rates
- **Pain Points**: Scaling outreach, SDR turnover
- **Approach**: Emphasize pipeline quality and team efficiency

## Marketing Leaders
- **Priorities**: Brand consistency, multi-channel campaigns
- **Pain Points**: Limited personalization bandwidth
- **Approach**: Highlight personalization at scale capabilities

## Consultants
- **Priorities**: High-value client acquisition
- **Pain Points**: Long sales cycles, too much admin work
- **Approach**: Focus on premium positioning and time optimization

## Coaches
- **Priorities**: Personal connection, steady lead flow
- **Pain Points**: Feast/famine cycles
- **Approach**: Emphasize authentic connection and consistent pipeline

## Agencies
- **Priorities**: Scalable results for clients
- **Pain Points**: Manual campaign execution
- **Approach**: Multi-client management and automation benefits

## Recruiting Agencies
- **Priorities**: Faster placements, higher-quality pipelines
- **Pain Points**: Hours sourcing candidates, missed follow-ups
- **Approach**: Speed and candidate quality improvements

## Financial Services (Regulated)
- **Priorities**: Trust, compliance, credibility
- **Pain Points**: Regulatory risk, outreach constraints
- **Approach**: Compliance-first messaging and trust building

## Legal Services (Regulated)
- **Priorities**: Client origination, credibility
- **Pain Points**: Ethics rules, sensitive messaging
- **Approach**: Professional credibility and regulatory compliance

## Pharma / Healthcare (Regulated)
- **Priorities**: HCP engagement, compliant communications
- **Pain Points**: MLR reviews, strict approval cycles
- **Approach**: Streamlined compliance and faster approvals

## Manufacturing
- **Priorities**: Supply chain efficiency, market expansion
- **Pain Points**: Outreach bottlenecks, regional scaling
- **Approach**: Operational efficiency and geographic expansion
