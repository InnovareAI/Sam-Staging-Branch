# LinkedIn Sales Navigator Search Criteria - RAG Knowledge Base

## Overview
LinkedIn Sales Navigator provides 50+ search filters across 8 main categories to target prospects and companies. This comprehensive list serves as the foundational knowledge base for ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) configuration and prospect targeting.

## Lead Search Filters

### 1. Spotlight Filters (Timing & Intent Signals)
- **Changed Jobs**: Last 90 days - High-intent prospects evaluating new solutions
- **Posted on LinkedIn**: Recent activity - Engaged professionals more likely to respond
- **In the News**: Last 30 days - Professionals with increased visibility and decision-making power
- **Shared Content**: Recent posts and articles - Active thought leaders
- **Started New Position**: Recently promoted - New decision-making authority

### 2. Personal Details Filters
- **Geography**: Physical location (not company location)
  - Country-level targeting
  - State/province targeting  
  - City-level targeting
  - Regional clusters
- **Industry**: From individual LinkedIn profile (413+ industries available)
- **First Name**: Specific name targeting
- **Last Name**: Surname-based filtering
- **Primary Language**: Profile language setting
- **Years of Experience**: Total professional experience range
- **LinkedIn Groups**: Group membership targeting
- **School/University**: Educational background

### 3. Role & Experience Filters
- **Seniority Level**: Company hierarchy position
  - **C-Level**: CEO, CTO, CFO, CMO, CHRO, etc.
  - **VP**: Vice President level
  - **Director**: Director level
  - **Manager**: Manager level (Experienced Manager, Entry-level Manager)
  - **Individual Contributor**: Senior, Associate, Entry-level
  - **Owner**: Business owners, Partners
  - **Strategic**: Strategic roles and consultants
  
- **Function**: Department/role type
  - **Sales**: Sales, Business Development, Account Management
  - **Marketing**: Digital Marketing, Content Marketing, Brand Marketing
  - **Operations**: Operations, Supply Chain, Logistics
  - **Finance**: Finance, Accounting, FP&A
  - **Human Resources**: HR, Talent Acquisition, People Operations
  - **Engineering**: Software Engineering, Product Engineering
  - **Information Technology**: IT, Systems Administration, DevOps
  - **Consulting**: Management Consulting, Strategy Consulting
  - **Business Development**: Partnerships, Alliances
  - **Customer Success**: Customer Success, Customer Support
  - **Product Management**: Product, Product Marketing
  - **Legal**: Legal, Compliance, Risk Management
  - **Research**: R&D, Data Science, Analytics

- **Current Job Title**: Specific title targeting
- **Past Job Title**: Previous role targeting
- **Years at Current Company**: Tenure at current organization
- **Years in Current Position**: Time in current role

### 4. Relationship Filters
- **Connection Degree**: 1st, 2nd, 3rd degree connections
- **Mutual Connections**: Shared network contacts
- **TeamLink**: Connections through team members
- **Contact Information**: Available contact details
- **CRM Status**: Integration with CRM systems (Enterprise feature)

## Search Strategy Best Practices

### 1. Intent Signal Prioritization
1. **Changed Jobs** (highest intent)
2. **Department Growth** (budget availability)
3. **Job Postings** (hiring = growth)
4. **Recent LinkedIn Activity** (engagement)
5. **Company in News** (momentum)

### 2. ICP Optimization Framework
1. **Define Core Criteria**: Must-have attributes
2. **Add Growth Signals**: Expansion indicators
3. **Include Intent Signals**: Timing factors
4. **Layer Technology Filters**: Current tech stack
5. **Refine Geography**: Market focus areas

### 3. Search Refinement Process
1. **Start Broad**: Use core ICP criteria
2. **Add Filters Gradually**: Refine results
3. **Monitor Result Quality**: Track response rates
4. **Adjust Based on Performance**: Optimize filters
5. **Save High-Performing Searches**: Create alerts

## Filter Limitations & Considerations

### 1. Known Issues
- **Seniority Algorithm**: LinkedIn's algorithm can misclassify roles
- **Industry Mismatch**: Personal vs company industry confusion
- **Geographic Accuracy**: Location vs company headquarters
- **Technology Data**: May not reflect current usage

### 2. Optimization Tips
- **Combine Multiple Filters**: Use 3-5 filters for precision
- **Test Filter Combinations**: A/B test different approaches
- **Regular Review**: Update filters based on market changes
- **Performance Monitoring**: Track and optimize continuously
