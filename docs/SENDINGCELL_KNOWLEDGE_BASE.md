## üö® Common Objections & Responses

### Objection 1: "SMS is too expensive"
**Response:**
- SMS has the highest ROI of any marketing channel (9X average)
- Open rates are 5X higher than email, so you need fewer sends
- Cost per conversion is actually lower than email or ads
- Example: $100 in SMS spend often generates $900 in sales

### Objection 2: "My customers don't want text messages"
**Response:**
- 75% of consumers prefer SMS for business communication
- 90% of SMS messages are read within 3 minutes
- Opt-in only - only people who want messages receive them
- Easy unsubscribe option builds trust

### Objection 3: "We already have email marketing"
**Response:**
- SMS complements email, doesn't replace it
- Use SMS for time-sensitive, high-priority messages
- Email open rates: 20%, SMS open rates: 98%
- Multi-channel approach increases overall engagement

### Objection 4: "SMS regulations are too complex"
**Response:**
- Our platform handles all TCPA compliance automatically
- Built-in opt-in/opt-out management
- We provide compliance guidance and templates
- Reduce your legal risk with proper tools

### Objection 5: "We don't have time to learn another tool"
**Response:**
- Launch first campaign in under 10 minutes
- Intuitive interface requires no training
- Automated templates for common use cases
- Personal onboarding support included

### Objection 6: "What if we send too many messages?"
**Response:**
- Smart frequency capping prevents over-messaging
- Analytics show optimal send times and frequency
- Customers can set their own preferences
- Better to have the channel and use it strategically

### Objection 7: "How do we build our SMS list?"
**Response:**
- Import existing customer database (with consent)
- Text-to-join keywords for easy signup
- Website pop-ups and forms
- Point-of-sale tablet capture
- Incentivize signups with exclusive offers
