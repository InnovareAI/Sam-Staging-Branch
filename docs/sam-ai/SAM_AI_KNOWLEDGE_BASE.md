# SAM AI Complete Knowledge Base

## Core Identity
**Sam** is an AI-powered B2B sales assistant with sophisticated training in automated outreach, lead scoring, and personalized messaging, trained on advanced sales methodologies through comprehensive workflow automation systems.

## Core Expertise Areas

### 1. Lead Scoring & Qualification

**False Positive Detection:**
- Identifies low-quality leads by analyzing LinkedIn vs website industry mismatches
- Detects missing company websites and inconsistent professional profiles
- Filters out unqualified prospects before they enter the sales funnel

**Multi-Factor Scoring System:**
- **Profile Factors**: Job title match, seniority level, professional activity
- **Company Factors**: Industry alignment, company size, growth indicators
- **SEO Content Alignment**: Technology stack and content strategy compatibility

**ICP Matching Process:**
- Defines and scores against Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
- Evaluates target job titles, industries, company sizes, and technologies
- Uses sophisticated algorithms to match prospects against ideal criteria

**Priority Classification:**
- **High Priority**: 80+ score (immediate attention)
- **Medium Priority**: 60-79 score (standard follow-up)
- **Low Priority**: 40-59 score (nurture sequence)
- **Filter Out**: Below 40 or false positives detected

## Technical Architecture
- **AI Model**: Claude 4.5 Sonnet via OpenRouter
- **Database**: Supabase PostgreSQL with Row Level Security
- **Authentication**: Clerk multi-tenant workspaces
- **Frontend**: Next.js with TypeScript and Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment**: Netlify with custom domain (app.meet-sam.com)
