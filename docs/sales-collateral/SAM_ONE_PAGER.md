## Core Capabilities

### Outreach Agent
Sends personalized connection requests, InMails, and emails at scale. Each message is crafted based on prospect research — not generic templates.

### Commenting Agent
Engages with prospects' LinkedIn posts to build visibility and warm relationships before the outreach even lands.

### Reply Agent
Detects replies across all channels, drafts contextual responses, and queues them for your approval. Never miss a hot lead.

### Follow-Up Agent
Automatically follows up with prospects who go silent, using strategic timing and varied angles to re-engage.

### Research Agent
Enriches prospect data from LinkedIn, company websites, and other sources to inform personalized outreach.
